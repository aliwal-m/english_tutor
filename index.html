Hello! My name is Maria

✅I’ve been teaching English to kids and teens for over 10 years.

✅I’ve worked as a private tutor since 2013.

✅I completed training programs Jolly Phonics, TEFL, CELTA, TKT_1, TKT_2.

✅I teach online, using Zoom, interactive whiteboards, games, and creative projects.


How We Learn

✔️I use the Communicative Approach, so children start speaking English from the very first lesson.

✔️Lessons are based on popular coursebooks such as Kid’s Box and Go Getter — every class like a small adventure.

✔️We read bright, age-appropriate stories aloud and discuss them.

✔️We learn through games, songs, and creative activities.

✔️We focus on understanding and communication.

What Your Child Will Gain

❤️Confidence when speaking English.

❤️The ability to build simple sentences and read short texts.

❤️A positive attitude toward learning English — without fear or stress.
